**New releases**

On July 8th, we released [Vidalia 0.1.15.](https://blog.torproject.org/blog/vidalia-0115-released).

On July 8th, we released [Tor 0.2.1.17-rc](https://blog.torproject.org/blog/tor-02117rc-released).
